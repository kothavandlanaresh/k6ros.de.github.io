The Stiffness Matrix: Fundamentals and Examples

Introduction

The stiffness matrix is a key concept in FEM representing the relationship between forces and displacements in a structure. It is fundamental for solving structural problems.

Applications

The stiffness matrix is used to solve equations of motion, determine displacements, and assess stresses in structural analysis.

Summary

The stiffness matrix is crucial for understanding how a structure will respond to applied forces, and it's fundamental in FEM analysis.
