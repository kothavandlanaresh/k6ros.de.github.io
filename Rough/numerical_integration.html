What is Numerical Integration?

Numerical integration, also known as quadrature, is used in FEM to compute element matrices. It allows for approximate integration of functions over an element's domain.

Why is Numerical Integration Important in FEM?

Numerical integration is used to evaluate element matrices and vectors efficiently, ensuring accurate solutions without needing exact analytic forms.
