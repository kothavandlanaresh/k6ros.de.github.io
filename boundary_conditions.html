Boundary Conditions in Finite Element Method

Learn the importance of boundary conditions in FEM.

What are Boundary Conditions?

Boundary conditions are constraints imposed on a system in FEM to simulate how the structure interacts with its surroundings.

Types of Boundary Conditions

  • Dirichlet Boundary Conditions (displacement boundary conditions)
  • Neumann Boundary Conditions (force boundary conditions)
  • Mixed Boundary Conditions

How to Apply Boundary Conditions in FEM?

In FEM, boundary conditions are applied at nodes, and the system's degrees of freedom are adjusted based on these conditions to simulate realistic scenarios.
