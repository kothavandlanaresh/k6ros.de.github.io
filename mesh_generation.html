Mesh Generation in Finite Element Method

Learn how mesh generation works in FEM.

What is Mesh Generation?

Mesh generation is the process of subdividing a complex domain into smaller, simpler shapes (elements), such as triangles or quadrilaterals, that make up the mesh in FEM analysis.

Types of Meshes

  • Structured Mesh
  • Unstructured Mesh
  • Hybrid Mesh

Importance of Mesh Quality

A good quality mesh is crucial for accurate results. Poor mesh quality can lead to inaccurate solutions and higher computational costs.
